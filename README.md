# 📊 Sales & Customer Tableau Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze sales performance and customer behavior through KPI-driven visualizations and dynamic filtering.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key sales metrics, identify trends, evaluate product performance, and analyze customer purchasing behavior for informed decision-making.

---

# 📌 Dashboard Pages

## 1. Sales Dashboard

Provides an executive overview of overall sales performance.

### Highlights

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity
- Sales vs Previous Year
- Highest & Lowest Sales Month
- Sales and Profit by Subcategory
- Weekly Sales & Profit Trends
- Interactive Navigation & Filters

---

## 2. Customer Dashboard

Provides customer-centric insights to understand purchasing behavior and business growth.

### Highlights

- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Average Sales per Customer
- Customer Growth vs Previous Year
- Customer Distribution by Number of Orders
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Interactive Navigation & Filters

---

# ✨ Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI Cards
